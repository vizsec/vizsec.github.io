VizSec 2022

Welcome to 19th IEEE Symposium on Visualization for Cyber Security

VizSec 2022 will be held in conjunction with the hybrid IEEE VIS Conference on October 26, 2022 in Oklahoma City, USA and online. VizSec brings together researchers and practitioners in information visualization and security to address the specific needs of the cyber security community through new and insightful visualization techniques.

To register for VizSec, see the IEEE VIS registration page.

Questions? Please email chair@vizsec.org for questions regarding VizSec 2022.
